Top 3 books I have read

  1. Harry Potter Series by J.K. Rowling

    2. The magical world of Hogwarts and the adventures of Harry, Hermione, and Ron are timeless.
    Rowling's ability to craft a detailed and immersive universe filled with relatable characters,
    moral lessons, and the battle between good and evil makes this series a classic for all ages.
    Its blend of fantasy, friendship, and courage continues to captivate readers worldwide.

  2. The Girl in Room 105 by Chetan Bhagat

    Bhagat weaves a gripping tale of love, suspense, and contemporary issues in "The Girl in Room 105."
    Set against the backdrop of modern India, the story follows a jilted lover's quest to solve a mystery.
    The book touches upon societal challenges while keeping readers engaged with its fast-paced narrative
    and unexpected twists.

  3. The Lost Symbol by Dan Brown

    Dan Brown's symbologist protagonist, Robert Langdon, returns in an exhilarating thriller that delves into
    the world of secret societies and hidden symbols. Set in Washington D.C., the book combines historical
    references with a suspenseful plot, taking readers on a thrilling journey through Masonic secrets and ancient mysteries.
    It's a page-turner that keeps the reader on edge until the very end.
